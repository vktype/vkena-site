# ✦ GenP Guide
Easily download and patch the latest version of any Creative Cloud software on **Windows** using **GenP**, an unlimited selection of apps, faster updates, and other benefits.

1. Download [Adobe Creative Cloud](https://helpx.adobe.com/download-install/apps/download-install-apps/creative-cloud-apps/download-creative-cloud-desktop-app-from-web.html). You must create an account and sign in. Feel free to use a temporary email. Do **not** install Adobe Genuine Service (AGS) *if prompted*.
2. Go to **Menu** > **File** > **Preferences** and in the **General** tab, disable the following settings: 
<div class="indent">

*Launch Creative Cloud at login*

*Run Creative Cloud in the background after closing*

*Automatically keep Creative Cloud up to date*

</div>

3. Close **Creative Cloud** once finished. *(**Menu** > **File** > **Quit Creative Cloud**)*
4. Download GenP from the [official GenP website](https://gen.paramore.su/).
The Binary download is recommended for beginners as it’s ready to use, while source is intended for more advanced users.
<span class="note">Windows Defender, along with certain third-party programs, are known to wrongfully block GenP. You must whitelist or exclude the file. You may also disable real-time protection completely, but do not forget to turn it back on afterwards.</span>
5. Right-click the zip > **Extract all** *(GenP.vX.X.X-CGP.zip)* and **Run GenP** *(GenP-vX.X.X.exe)* **as administrator**.
6. In the main tab, click **Search**. You should be prompted three files underneath **Creative Cloud**, ensure that all three are selected and click **Patch**.
7. Open **Creative Cloud** and install your desired applications *(Premiere, After Effects, and so on)* in the **Apps** menu. Once the applications are installed, close **Creative Cloud** and all respective **Adobe** apps.
8. **Run GenP** *(GenP-vX.X.X.exe)* **as administrator** and click **Search**. This time, select the files for your program(s) *(Premiere, After Effects, etc)* and click **Patch**.
The application will now be patched, enjoy.
